Quality

Users can either upvote a post they like or downvote one they don't. The downvoted items will sink, while the upvoted items will float. This ensures you only see posts that you are most likely enjoy.

Relevance

Posts can only be marked by one tag at a time. This leads to posts that are more focused on one significant topic. Users can follow multiple tags leading to a variety of information, but only on the topics you care about.

Discovery

Users can also be tagged so you can follow both the tags you enjoy and the users that you find most interesting.
